Dissertation

Title: The Impact of Artificial Intelligence on Digital Content Creation: Transforming Mauritius's Advertising Landscape

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is reshaping industries globally, and advertising is at the forefront of this transformation. AI-powered tools are helping businesses work more efficiently, customize their campaigns, and produce groundbreaking content, revolutionizing the way advertising operates (Gao et al., 2023). However, this innovation comes with its own set of challenges. Critics raise concerns about AI's potential to overshadow human creativity and the ethical issues surrounding automated decision-making (Yu, 2022). Generative AI, which includes technologies like natural language processing (NLP) and image generation, has introduced powerful tools for advertisers. These technologies can speed up content creation and allow professionals to focus on more strategic, high-level tasks. While some see AI as a valuable creative partner, others worry it may lead to a loss of personal touch and originality in the creative process (Eaton, 2020). This dissertation dives into the impact of AI on advertising, with a particular focus on Mauritius. The study explores how local advertisers are adopting generative AI, the challenges they face, and the ethical questions these technologies raise. By doing so, it aims to offer practical insights that can help advertising professionals in Mauritius navigate this rapidly evolving landscape (Spajić et al., 2023).

Chapter 1

Intro

Globally, the advertising industry is undergoing significant changes, fueled by the rapid growth of AI technologies. By 2025, global spending on AI is predicted to surpass $500 billion, highlighting its enormous influence on creative processes (Yu, 2022). AI is revolutionizing tasks like content organization and ad optimization, but it also raises questions about its effects on creativity and ethical decision-making (Cohen, 2024). In Mauritius, a growing hub for advertising, the adoption of AI presents both opportunities and challenges. Many agencies still rely on traditional methods and face limited resources, which can make it difficult to keep up with global competitors. However, AI has the potential to level the playing field, giving smaller agencies access to powerful creative tools that were once reserved for larger firms. Generative AI technologies, like ChatGPT and DALL·E 2, are already changing advertising practices worldwide by providing fast, cost-effective solutions for content creation (Gao et al., 2023). These tools can help professionals overcome creative roadblocks, deliver more personalized content, and optimize their campaigns. Yet, little research has been done on how these technologies are being adopted in Mauritius and what their impact looks like in this context. This study addresses that gap by exploring how AI is transforming the advertising landscape in Mauritius. It looks at how AI can enhance creativity, improve efficiency, and tackle ethical concerns. By connecting global advancements to local realities, the research offers a roadmap to help Mauritian advertisers embrace AI’s potential while navigating its challenges.
